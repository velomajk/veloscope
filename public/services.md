# Services & Support

At Veloscope, we build focused, lightweight, and effective apps for the Atlassian Ecosystem. Our goal is to fill the gaps in native Jira reporting to help Scrum Masters and Product Owners visualize progress instantly.

## Our Solutions

### Atlassian Marketplace Apps
Our flagship solutions are designed to provide better visibility into your Sprints and Epics.

* **Sprint Visualization:** Clear metrics on Story Points (SP), work remaining, and team velocity.
* **Dashboard Gadgets:** Drag-and-drop gadgets that give you a high-level view of project health without complex configuration.
* **Plug & Play:** Easy installation with standard Jira configuration.
